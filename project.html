Parade of the planet 2025

Six of the planets in our solar system will line up, in what is sometimes called a 'planetary parade'. They include Venus, Jupiter and Saturn and Mars which can all be seen in the sky with the naked eye. Uranus and Neptune will also be part of the line-up, but you'll need a telescope to spot them.
